1. Rinse the fish and pat dry
2. Rub mayo on one side
3. Season with salt and pepper and Old Bay if desired
4. Sprinkle with potato flakes
5. Heat olive oil on medium heat
6. Add sea bass with the mayo side on top
7. Cook for 7 minutes
8. Flip mayo side of sea bass on bottom
9. Cook for 8 minutes
10. Flip and cook 1-2 minutes longer depending on desired doneness and thickness of fish
11. Splash with lemon and garnish with parsley
